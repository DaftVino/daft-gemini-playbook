# 025 — Employee Investigation Intake Organizer

- **Category:** Payroll, HR & Compliance
- **Surface:** Gemini in Docs
- **Risk:** Red
- **Owner:** HR lead
- **Last reviewed:** 2026-07-21
- **Required sources:** @Complaint or Report, @Messages, @Schedule or Time Records, @Policy
- **Core blocks:** Evidence, Legal/People/Risk

## When to use

A complaint has been received and the first hour matters — before evidence moves and before anyone
forms a view.

## Prompt

```text
Organize @Complaint or Report, @Messages, @Schedule or Time Records, and @Policy for [matter].

Return, in neutral language throughout:
(1) The allegation, stated as reported — not as characterized
(2) Parties and their roles
(3) A known timeline, with the source for each entry, and gaps marked as gaps
(4) Evidence currently available
(5) Witnesses and records to preserve, with who must preserve each and by when
(6) Information gaps — what is not yet known
(7) Immediate concerns requiring a decision now: safety, retaliation risk, operational continuity,
    separation of the parties
(8) Neutral next investigation steps, in order
(9) The policy provisions that may be relevant, cited by section

Do not make credibility findings. Do not assess whether the allegation is substantiated. Do not
recommend discipline or any employment action. Do not characterize any party's conduct or motive.
Use "reported that" rather than asserting. Flag every point requiring HR and counsel review. Where
the materials do not establish a fact, leave it in the gaps list — state "Insufficient evidence to
conclude" rather than reconstructing what probably happened.
```

## Validation before use

- **Authorized reviewer: HR lead, with counsel where the matter involves harassment,
  discrimination, retaliation, safety, or potential termination.**
- Evidence preservation is the time-critical output. Act on section 5 immediately.
- This document is discoverable. Nothing speculative, characterizing, or conclusory belongs in it —
  read it once specifically for that before saving.
- Restrict access to HR and counsel. Do not store it in a shared operational folder.

## Example follow-up

`List only the preservation actions and who must complete each in the next 24 hours.`
